Parliamentary Representation of EU Member States from 1986 to 2014

by Ben Isaacs

About this map:

This map illustrates the change in parliamentary representation in the European Union between the years 1986 and 2014. As the user sequences through the map's time intervals, the symbols grow and shrink to represent the change in their respective countries' parliamentary representation. Some years, new symbols appear altogether, representing the admission of new states into the union.

The data for this map comes from the European Election Database of Norsk Senter for Forskningsdata (NSD).
